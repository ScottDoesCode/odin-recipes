Chicken Curry

Balti Chicken Recipe

This chicken curry recipe features tender chicken simmered in a rich, aromatic sauce made with onions, garlic, ginger, and a blend of spices like turmeric, cumin, and garam masala. The addition of tomatoes and coconut milk creates a creamy, flavorful base, while fresh cilantro adds a burst of freshness. Served with rice or naan, it's a comforting and satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (boneless or bone-in, cut into pieces)
  • Onions (finely chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Ginger (grated or minced)
  • Tomatoes (chopped or pureed)
  • Coconut milk or heavy cream (for creaminess)
  • Vegetable oil or ghee
  • Lemon juice (optional for a tangy flavor)

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Finely chop the onion and mince the garlic and ginger.
  3. Chop the tomatoes (or use canned pureed tomatoes).
  4. In a large pan or skillet, heat 2 tbsp of oil or ghee over medium heat.
  5. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they become soft and golden brown, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Add the minced garlic and ginger to the onions. Stir and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  7. Stir in the chopped or pureed tomatoes. Cook for about 5-7 minutes until the tomatoes soften and form a thick sauce.
  8. Add the chicken pieces to the pan, stirring to coat them with the spice mixture. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the chicken is lightly browned on all sides.
  9. Taste the curry and adjust seasoning if needed, adding more salt, chili powder, or garam masala as desired.
